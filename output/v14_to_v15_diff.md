## 0. v15 changeset summary (in one paragraph)

v15 adds ONE new structural upgrade: **Intra-Cluster Diversification (ICD)** via NEW step 05.45 (between v14 step 05.4 and v12 step 05.5). Step 05.45 computes each of the 25 k-means-selected candidates' cosine similarity to EVERY prior corpus INVESTIGATIVE_CANDIDATE (cached embeddings in NEW `logs/investigative_cluster_state.json`) and filters out candidates within `CLUSTER_PROXIMITY_THRESHOLD = 0.60`. Primary candidate selection is replaced from v14's "first to pass step 05.5" to "filtered-25's first to pass step 05.5; fallback to v14 rule if filter empties". The post-epoch wrapper `output/15_intra_cluster_diversity_E{N}.json` reports the cross-investigative ablation (subsumes option c at step 14.5). All upgrades are STRICTLY ADDITIVE — they do not modify any FORBIDDEN-TO-MODIFY zone (which now includes v14's step 05.4 k-means filter, step 14.5 coverage profile, step 13.5 attack, step 13 spec, step 14 coherence, step 10 mechanical verdict, step 12 tree-stream, step 07 kw, step 06 web_search, plus v12 step 05.5). ## 5. Score formula diff

v14 score formula (`score_v14`) is preserved. v15 adds FIVE new terms:

```diff
score_v15 = score_v14
+ + ((1 − cluster_proximity_to_prior_investigative_mean) × 5)   // v15 ICD primary
+ + (filter_pass_rate × 3)                                       // v15 ICD
+ + (corpus_unique_investigative_niches / 5 × 4)                 // v15 ICD (normalized; cap at 5 niches)
+ + (epoch_passes_diversification × 2)                           // v15 ICD binary
+ − (mean_primary_candidate_cluster_proximity_max × 3)           // v15 ICD penalty
```

Where score_v14 contains all v1-v14 terms (verbatim from program_v14.md §6).

Net v15 contribution range: -3 to +14 (depending on E34 outcomes).
- Best case: cluster_proximity_mean=0.30, filter_pass_rate=0.80, unique_niches=3, epoch_passes_diversification=true, primary_proximity_max_mean=0.30 → contributes (1-0.30)*5 + 0.80*3 + 3/5*4 + 1*2 + (-0.30*3) = 3.5 + 2.4 + 2.4 + 2 - 0.9 = +9.4.
- Worst case (no diversification): cluster_proximity_mean=0.65, filter_pass_rate=0.10, unique_niches=1, epoch_passes_diversification=false, primary_proximity_max_mean=0.65 → (1-0.65)*5 + 0.10*3 + 1/5*4 + 0 - 0.65*3 = 1.75 + 0.3 + 0.8 + 0 - 1.95 = +0.9.

## 8. Why this is the right v15

The cluster audit (`output/investigative_cluster_audit.md`) shows the v14 limitation is **the generator over-mines one latent region** ("algebraic-structure-equipped learnable module with non-trivial commutator invariant"). The 7 corpus INVESTIGATIVE candidates form ONE cluster at all reasonable thresholds; ALL use the same `variant_equivalence` attack category; ALL use the same "training penalty maintains non-trivial structure" rebuttal template.

v15 is the ONLY option that DIRECTLY addresses this limitation at the generation level. The other options:
- (b) Runnable toy spec: downstream validation tool; doesn't change candidate distribution.
- (c) Cross-investigative ablation: diagnostic only; doesn't generate new candidates.
- (d) Reverse-engineer step 10: orthogonal axis; doesn't address the cluster problem.

v15 subsumes (c) as a sub-feature (`output/15_intra_cluster_diversity_E{N}.json` IS the structural-duplicate flag report). v15 leaves (b) for v16 (once v15 has surfaced multi-cluster INVESTIGATIVE coverage, per-candidate empirical priority becomes more useful) and (d) for v17+ (once exploration-diversity is maxed out, can switch to step-10 boundary work).

This is the right v15 because it is the OPTION DIRECTLY EVIDENCED by the Phase-1 cluster audit. The cluster audit motivates the choice; the choice addresses the cluster.
