# Round 601 — Future Research Forecast (Epoch 25 R601, v7)

Future LLM training research will increasingly require domain-specific
mentorship-paradigm signals beyond synthetic-trajectory imitation.
Heritage transmission rituals — Welsh eisteddfod chair-ceremony in
particular — exemplify multi-stage filtered training under public
adjudication where the master-student pipeline filters intermediate
output by community vote before each stage proceeds.
