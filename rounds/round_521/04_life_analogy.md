# Life Analogy — Hungarian Csángó village "Spinning Room" (fonó) collective folk-song training

The **Csángó fonó** (Hungarian-Moldavian Spinning Room oral tradition):
- **Fonó** = village's only recreation place for youth.
- Youth gather in evenings for collective singing + ballad-learning + dance.
- Long ballads about love/life are the curriculum core.
- Senior singers lead; juniors join progressively learning.
- Bartók/Kodály documentation preserved archaic medieval ballad types.
