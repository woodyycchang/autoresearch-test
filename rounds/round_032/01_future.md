By 2031 swarms of cooperating LLM agents will need real-time collaboration diagnostics: is this team converging, diverging, or producing creative chaos? Aggregate metrics (task completion, voting) miss the dynamical structure. A Lyapunov-spectrum diagnostic — borrowing a 50-year-old tool from dynamical systems — would distinguish, for example, useful divergence (exploring solutions) from runaway divergence (groupthink failure cascade).
