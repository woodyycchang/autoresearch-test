# Life Analogy — Anangu tjukurpa songline land-curriculum

The **Anangu Pitjantjatjara tjukurpa**:
- Songlines = creator-ancestor paths across land + sky; each waypoint encodes landmarks + waterholes.
- Tjukurpa learning is hierarchical: child level → adolescent → mature initiate.
- Knowledge is transmitted as ORDERED TRAVERSAL of waypoints — walking the songline IS the curriculum.

**SONGLINE-CURRICULUM**: a structured-trajectory continual-learning schedule where training data is ordered as an explicit spatial-knowledge graph G = (waypoints, edges) with hierarchical-difficulty annotation; replay schedule traverses the graph from "novice" waypoints outward (curriculum-by-trajectory) rather than randomly. Combines (a) curriculum learning order with (b) explicit waypoint-graph traversal replay.
