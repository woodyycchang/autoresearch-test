## Scan focus
Mean field game (MFG) theory as mechanism source for multi-agent LLM-agent coordination. MFG: continuum of agents with state distribution ρ(t,x); each agent optimizes against ρ; equilibrium is a coupled HJB-Fokker-Planck system. The candidate transfers MFG equilibrium concept to multi-LLM agent populations.

## Motivation
mechanism_transfer: MFG IS the rigorous N→∞ limit of N-agent stochastic games with mean-field interaction. Recent work uses MFG for multi-agent RL and LLM-agent populations. The candidate proposes computing MFG-equilibrium policy as the reference for individual LLM-agent updates.
