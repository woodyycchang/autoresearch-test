A chemical reaction at equilibrium: rate forward = k_f × [A] × [B], rate backward = k_b × [C] × [D], net = 0. Perturbing one concentration shifts the equilibrium per Le Chatelier. In LLM-MAS analog: rate of useful-task-completion forward = k × [capability_per_agent] × [num_agents]; rate of cost-incurral backward = k' × [cost_per_agent] × [num_agents]; equilibrium is where the net production is zero or maximized.
