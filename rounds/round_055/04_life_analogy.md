A person is detained without explanation; the court issues a writ; the jailer must produce the body and the legal basis for detention within a fixed window. If the jailer cannot produce or the legal basis fails, the person is released. Not because the jailer's reasons sound weak — the standard for production is exogenous and binary. An LLM agent in a high-stakes audit needs the same structural rigor: production is non-optional, justification standard is exogenous, remedy is automatic.
