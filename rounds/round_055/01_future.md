By 2031 agents will be embedded in high-stakes decision pipelines (medical, legal, financial) where 'show your work and justify' is not optional. Today's chain-of-thought reasoning is voluntary inspection by the user; the agent can omit, summarize, or rationalize post-hoc. A forced-production protocol with exogenous statutory standards (akin to habeas corpus) would close this loophole: the agent cannot refuse to produce, the standard for sufficient justification is not under the agent's control, and the remedy for insufficient justification is automatic retraction.
