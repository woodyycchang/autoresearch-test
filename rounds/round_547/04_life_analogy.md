- No weight classes; no time limits; single-elimination tournament structure.
- Win condition: opponent's back, knee, OR elbow touches ground = 3-touch fall.
- No leg-grabs, no ground-grappling allowed.
- 9-rank title ladder: arslan (lion), zaan (elephant), garid (hawk), avraga (titan).
- Eagle dance (devekh) before/after match — shamanistic ritual.
