Think of how a pop-up Slack reminder works. The reminder is not just data ("at 3pm meet John") — it is the act itself: the shape of the notification automatically opens at 3pm, automatically pings the user, automatically becomes the foreground concern. The data and the actuator are the same artifact; there is no separate "decision module" that reads the reminder text and decides to interrupt. Configure the reminder, the behavior follows directly from the structural form of the configuration. Compare to a calendar text file that just sits there: same information, no automatic actuation.
