Think of how a long-running file folder of meeting notes naturally evolves. The first time you reach for last quarter's project notes, you write a quick summary at the top because the original is too long. The next time, you reach for the summary and add to it. Over months, the original detailed notes recede; the summary thickens. Small ad-hoc one-page memos get absorbed into the main project summary. By year end, you have a few large mature documents and very few small one-offs. The folder coarsens itself through repeated re-handling, with size growing in proportion to past handling.
