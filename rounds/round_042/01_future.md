By 2031, multi-agent LLM systems will be deployed at population scales (1000+ agents) where individual failures can cascade. Form A: the joint mechanism of *trophic cascade* (ecosystem ecology) AND *percolation threshold* (network physics) provides a quantitative predictor of cascade-amplification. Neither alone is sufficient: trophic cascade gives directional/topological structure; percolation gives threshold structure. The joint mechanism predicts that LLM-MAS cascades follow a sharp percolation-style transition above a critical connectivity, with cascade amplification scaling as in trophic-network ecology.
