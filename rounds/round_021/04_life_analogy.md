Think of how a single sheet of music can be performed two ways depending on a small notation: a flat sign at the start of a phrase shifts the entire melody half a step. The notes on the page are identical; the small structural marker (the accidental) tells the performer to read them under a different harmonic frame. The same content produces a different musical output, deterministically, based on a tiny cue. The audience doesn't need to recompute the notes; the cue at the start does the work.
