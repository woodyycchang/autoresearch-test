By 2031, LLM tool-use proficiency will be plottable as a continuous function of training compute, with thresholds at which specific tool-categories (search, code execution, browser, calculator) emerge. The Form-C candidate proposes a specific quantitative scaling law: tool-use accuracy on standard benchmarks scales as compute^γ with γ = 0.18 ± 0.05 across model families, with a sharp transition at compute = 10^22 FLOPs.
